Welcome to the Rail Operations & Maintenance Optimizer

This application helps optimize the operational planning of rail vehicle traffic and maintenance over a 14-day horizon. It considers shift-level granularity, assigns vehicles to predefined routes, and schedules maintenance activities.

The optimizer handles both preventive maintenance (optimizing scheduling around optimal km) and corrective maintenance (ensuring completion before a max km deadline), considering depot capabilities and vehicle routing needs.
